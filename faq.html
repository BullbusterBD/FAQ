Our lighter braid (40Lb and below) we make a four strand braid. The reason we make our lighter braids a 4 strand braid is because our lighter lines have thinner diameter so the most important factor for them is abrasion resistance. We want our thicker diameter lines to perform well, performance means handling and castability. Our braid between 50LB and 200Lb is 8 strand braid. This thicker diameter braid does not have a problem with abrasion resistance because the material that makes up each strand is much thicker than our lighter braids.
